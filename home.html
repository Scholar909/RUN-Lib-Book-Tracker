Library Rules

Do's

  • Return books on time.
  • Handle books with care.
  • Fill burrower details accurately.
  • Update return dates as needed.
  • Encourage others to donate old books.

Don'ts

  • Don’t borrow more than 3 books at once.
  • Don’t archive a book twice.
  • Don’t give false borrower data.
  • Don’t delete records without reason.
  • Don’t share private book links without permission.

Rules

  • An individual is allowed to borrow a maximum of three books at once. The duration of return is One Month.
  • The duration of return for two books is 3 weeks.
  • The duration of return for one book is 2 weeks.
  • The borrower is expected to fill in the correct details required by the Librarian(s) at the point of borrowing.
  • Failure to return the borrowed book(s) on or before the due return date without communication of reason(s) will incur a fine of ₦200 per week of extension.
  • An extension of return date can be made to any of the librarians via a WhatsApp message, phone call, or direct visit to the Library.
  • Any tear or damage on returned book(s) will incur a fine of ₦300 upwards on each book based on the extent of damage, at the discretion of the Librarian(s).
  • In case of loss of borrowed book(s), the borrower is expected to gift the department a brand new book(s) of the type borrowed and lost.
  • For any other enquiries regarding borrowed books or the library unit, you can contact the Head of Library on WhatsApp or call on 08122984954.
