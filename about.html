Help & About

Welcome, Library Member

This website is exclusively for registered library members. If you're viewing this page, you are successfully logged in and have access to BookRun’s record-keeping system.

What You Can Do

BookRun is designed to help manage and maintain accurate records within the library. As a member, you can:

  • Record and view borrowed books and borrower details.
  • Maintain a log of all available books in the library — whether on shelf, archived, or borrowed.
  • Track inventory and update book statuses as needed.

Important Guidelines

  • **No Recovery for Deleted Data**: Once an item is deleted, it is permanently removed. Please be absolutely sure before deleting anything.
  • **Editing Limitations**: Some records can be edited, others cannot. Take care to input accurate data from the start.
  • **Avoid Duplicates**: Double-entry of the same book or user record can disrupt the system. Always confirm before submitting.
  • **Proofread Entries**: Mistakes in spelling, formatting, or categorization can cause confusion. Always double-check your input.
